DOCTYPE

You'll need this tag at the beginning of every HTML document you create. It ensures that a broswer know that It's reading HTML. and that it expects HTML5. The latest version. Even though this isn't actully at HTML tag. it's still a good to know.


HTML

This is another tag that tell a broswer that it's read HTML. The html tag goes straight after the DOCTYPE tag. and you close it with a html tag at the end of your title everything else in your document goes between these tags.


Head

The Head tag starts the header section of your file. The stuff that goes in here doesn't appear of your website instead. It contains metadata for search engines and info for your broswer.'
